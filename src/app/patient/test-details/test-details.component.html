About the Test

This acid is a waste product that comes from the breakdown of serotonin by the liver. Higher levels of 5-HIAA in urine indicates carcinoid tumor.These tumors secrete serotonin. Carcinoid tumors can occur anywhere such as the intestinal tract the lungs appendix and other body tissues. A tumor marker protein chromogranin A may be ordered as a blood test.
