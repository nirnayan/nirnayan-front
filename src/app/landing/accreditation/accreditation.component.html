National Accreditation Board For Testing & Calibrations Laboratory

Fastest NABL(National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories)-accredited Laboratory in Eastern India according to our rendition, dainty, and quality check. An autonomous entity working under the Dept. of Science and Technology department of the Government of India is NABL. After our inauguration in 2018, we achieved this accreditation in 2019 for our daily operations.
