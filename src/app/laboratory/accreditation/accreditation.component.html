NABL

National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories

Fastest NABL (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories)-accredited Laboratory in Eastern India according to our rendition, dainty, and quality check. An autonomous entity working under the Dept. of Science and Technology department of the Government of India is NABL. After our inauguration in 2018, we achieved this accreditation in 2019 for our daily operations.

Govt. of India has authorized NABL as the sole accreditation body for testing and calibration laboratories.

Principles:

To get the accreditation we make a definite plan for our Quality Management System which is the spine of our laboratory to establish its recognition and performance that put an impact on our society.

  • Prepare Standard Operating Procedures are carried out in our lab to make each investigation important and impactful. 
  • Fully acquainted with all relevant documents and understand the assessment Procedure with expert pathologists.
  • Implementation of all aspects which are mentioned at the time of documentation.
  • Quality checking protocols for various levels put an extra accuracy for report generating.
  • Preparing of Quality Manual is the greatest advantage for our laboratory to achieve accreditation.

NABL is a signatory to the Asia Pacific Accreditation Cooperation (APAC) and International Laboratory Accreditation Cooperation (ILAC) through Mutual Recognition Arrangements (MRA). The laboratory’s compliance with the requirements of the standard and its technical competence is assessed by NABL for accreditation.

We are proud to announce that we have also been accredited by this in 2020. Designed our molecular department to execute the tests on a random basis along with supreme technology.

  • Availability of multiple functioning  PCR machines.
  • Availability of required experienced technologists with good handling of clinical specimens, especially respiratory samples.

  • Sufficient rendition for segregation and disposal of biomedical waste
  • Already having NABL accreditation to emphasize our performance.

ILAC MRA

International Laboratory Accreditation Cooperation Mutual Recognition Arrangement

ILAC MRA stands for International Laboratory Accreditation Cooperation Mutual Recognition Arrangement. An internationally recognized ‘stamp of approval’ to exhibit obedience against approved standards and requirements. This accreditation means that all our operations are as per the guidelines of ISO/IEC (ISO 15189), and meet international standards. This accreditation allows our globally connected testing portfolio, our calibrations in research works, and recognition in all aspects of our reliability and accuracy in results.  The MRA supports international trading by promoting international confidence and acceptance of data generated by our assurance and improvements.

ILAC is the global association for the accreditation of laboratories, assessment bodies, mastery testing providers, and reference material producers, with a membership consisting of accreditation bodies and stakeholder organizations throughout the world. ILAC
