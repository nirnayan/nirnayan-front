Whether you're curious about the services we offer, how to schedule an appointment, or what to bring for your visit, we have you covered. We hope that our FAQs will serve as a valuable resource, guiding you through the essential aspects of our healthcare services

How can I find information on tests and packages regarding a particular lab/ center?

You need to log in to www.nirnayan.com and click on the option of “Patient” and you will get the information about that.
How can I find the employee benefits of Our Healthcare?

You need to log in to www.nirnayan.com and then click on the “Career” option and there you will get the information about it.
I want to visit a Lab. How can I see the location and timing?

Log on to the website and you will get the option of “Locate us” and click on it. You will get the location details on it.
I want to know about the corporate partners of your lab and want to make a proposal to be a corporate partner. How do I connect with you?

You just need to log in to our Website and click on the “Association” button and you will get the corporate information about us. From there you can put your details to connect with us.
How can I get to know about your department's details?

You just need to log in to our website www.nirnayan.com and there you will get the option Laboratory click on it, and you will get the option Department. From there you will all the latest information about our departments.
