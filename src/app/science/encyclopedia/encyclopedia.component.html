What are kidneys?

Kidneys look like reddish beans and are situated at the sides of the spine. They contain fine, minute, net-like architecture (nephrons) that constantly filters out blood. By doing this, they are keeping the important substances inside the body, and removing the waste material.

The waste material leaves our body through urine. But that's not the only thing kidneys do. They fulfill some additional roles too.
